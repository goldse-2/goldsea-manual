    Q&A

    What cutting jobs is it suitable for?

    It is suitable for garden pruning, branch cutting, small wood cutting, camping and daily yard maintenance.

    Does it have automatic lubrication?

    Yes. The chain is automatically supplied with oil during use to help protect the blade and chain.

    Can the chain be adjusted without tools?

    Yes. Chain replacement and chain tension adjustment can be completed without special tools on the compatible models.

    How many batteries are included?

    The battery kit versions include two 2.0 Ah batteries that can be used alternately.

    How powerful is the motor?

    The mini chainsaw uses an 800W brushless motor designed for smooth cutting with lower noise.

    How many cuts can a full charge make?

    A full charge can make up to about 35 cuts, depending on wood size, hardness and operating conditions.
